Empowering Rural India on its path to digital and financial freedom

Maddox is Uttar Pradesh’s leading rural-digital service provider. We enable our network of rural stores to cater to the digital and financial needs of rural citizens in India.

We provide our stores complete access to a variety of digital ecommerce, utility bill payments, financial, banking and government services. Our modern and easy to use store dashboard helps our store understand and see their business grow easily and quickly.

Our Network comprises of more than 5,000 stores covering more than 10 million consumers throughout rural and semi urban Uttar Pradesh with access to all pin codes in the relevant areas.
